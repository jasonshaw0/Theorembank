Physics

Welcome to the Physics section. This area covers a wide range of physical laws and principles, from classical mechanics to modern quantum physics.

Subcategories

  • Mechanics
    • Kinematics (straight-line) - Motion of objects without considering forces.
    • Dynamics (forces & Newton's Laws) - Motion of objects under the influence of forces.
    • Rotational Mechanics - Motion of rotating bodies.
  • Thermodynamics
    • Laws of Thermodynamics & Entropy - Energy, heat, work, and disorder.
    • Gas Laws & Thermal Processes - Behavior of gases and heat transfer.
  • Electromagnetism
    • Electrostatics & Circuits - Electric charges, fields, and basic circuits.
    • Magnetism & Induction - Magnetic fields and electromagnetic induction.
  • Optics - Behavior and properties of light.
  • Waves & Oscillations - Periodic motion and wave phenomena.
  • Quantum Mechanics - Physics at atomic and subatomic levels.
  • Relativity
    • Special Relativity - Physics of high speeds and spacetime.
    • General Relativity - Gravity as a curvature of spacetime.
  • Fluid Mechanics - Behavior of fluids (liquids and gases).
  • Nuclear Physics - Structure and behavior of atomic nuclei.
