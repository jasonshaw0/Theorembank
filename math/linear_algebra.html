Dot Product

\(\mathbf{u}\cdot \mathbf{v} = u_x v_x + u_y v_y + u_z v_z = |\mathbf{u}|\;|\mathbf{v}|\cos\theta\)

Variables:

Description/Usage:

Produces a scalar equal to the sum of component-wise products. It measures how much one vector extends in the direction of another. If the dot product is zero, vectors are perpendicular (orthogonal).
